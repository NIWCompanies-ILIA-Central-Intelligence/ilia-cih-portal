The Vision

One platform. Every role. One intelligence.

The ILIA CIH Portal is a custom-branded AI workspace where NIW employees don't use Claude — they use NIW's platform, built on Claude. Each person logs in, gets their role-configured dashboard and AI assistant, and works entirely inside the NIW system.


Operations staff manage tickets and SOPs. Support staff answer client questions. Sales staff reference Kai-Zen strategy. Developers work with the codebase. All from the same platform. All connected to the same CIH intelligence layer. All with a login they own.
